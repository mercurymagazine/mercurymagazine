Comparing yourself to others - is there a good side to it?

This article explores the idea of comparing ourselves to others, and whether it is providing any benefits. Comparison is a natural part of human nature, and is something that we have always subconsciously sought to do. However, it is not always the best thing to do, and can poke holes in our self esteem.
